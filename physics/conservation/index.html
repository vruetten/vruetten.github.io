Law of action and reaction





We call the phenomenon, "exchanging of momentum”, as “force”-

I just finished talking about the definition of “force”, but “force” does not actually exist. It is just a concept that we use for the phenomenon when we observe a change in momentum, we then say that a “force is acting”. Do you feel you understand that?

For example, when two moving objects collide with each other, these objects must change their motion, but what we can observe is just the fact that motion has changed. We can not see the force, nor does force pop out. With observing the change of the motion, we just indirectly assume that “the force acted between these two objects”.

This just means that we decided to consider that “force— make momentum change” acts between objects. We just take the idea for granted.

we just call the phenomenon, "exchanging momentum”, as “force”

Then, why it was necessary to introduce this concept? The answer is that it is convenient and intuitive for human beings.” " She is forcing the pen to move.” “May the force with you” Those are common phrases we can hear in our dairy life. Those dairy terms and a physics’s concept, “force”, have some similarity.

If we use this concept, “force”, we can easy to summarize the effects among objects simply.

“An object, which exerts force, receives same amplitude of force with opposite direction.”

This is “low of action and reaction”. It is little bit hard to explain it without using the idea, “force”. In fact, conservation of momentum, which I will explain in next article, is the same concept as this.

Also, if the “force” is defined, it is useful for a field, “statics”, which is about mass center or balances of objects, and the idea is common in “electromagnetism”, too. The concept, “Force”, is applicable for broad area, and it is thoughtful that dynamics is translated as “learning of force” in Japanese. It is definitely true that “force” is the main theme of dynamics.

Dynamics, statics mechanics and kinematics are all translated to Japanese in same words. “Learning of force”. I don’t know how to use them properly, but it is better to follow the traditional way of expression in each fields. ———
